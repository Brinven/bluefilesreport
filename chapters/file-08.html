Power Transmission

An association between UFOs and electrical power facilities has been recognized. UFOs tend to hover near generating stations, switchyards, and substations, and travel along the rights-of-way for high-voltage transmission lines.

A direct relationship has been reported from numerous UFO sightings where the UFOs appeared at low altitude near the transmission lines, alternately rising and falling behind the trees. On more than one occasion, a "pipe-like or wire-like object came down from the base of the disc and actually touched the lines, remaining there for a minute or so.

Also UFOs have been seen around power stations during power blackouts. These blackouts are mostly blamed on a relay or switch. This type of relay is sensitive to stray radiation. The near by UFO would be emitting high-frequency electromagnetic radiation that would induce eddy currents in the disc of the relay disrupting its delicate balance and actuating a circuit breaker. Induced operation of the protective relays by electromagnetic radiation from UFOs is probably the reason for the power blackouts.
