Introduction

The topic of flying saucers and UFOs have been discussed throughout recorded human history. They have been seen around the world. Skeptics claim gravity manipulation and flying saucers are impossible. Yet history shows many things declared impossible — airplanes, space rockets, moon landings — until proven otherwise.

I am not a professional writer, but I have waited years for a book like this and felt compelled to create it. My goal is to highlight real witnesses and the work of other researchers by compiling and correlating scattered technical information from books, documentaries, magazines, websites, and podcasts.

I am no physicist. With limited formal education, I approach this as a seasoned investigator studying UFO cases, physics texts, and scientific papers. My hypotheses rest on verified facts and cutting-edge research from credible sources. I aim to address common questions and reveal technological similarities across reported craft.

Nuclear-powered systems fall under Department of Energy jurisdiction and are classified for national security, enabling government secrecy over advanced energy and propulsion technologies.

A full history of each craft would require separate volumes. Instead, I provide essential background while focusing on accessible explanations of the systems. Using modern scientific understanding, I connect known data with reasoned postulates to explain observed performance. Much of this information has been obscured by fragmentation; an open mind is essential.

My work in total will be demonstrating convergence — how independent sources, separated by decades, geography, and context, describe nearly identical systems using different language, different levels of technical understanding, and different frames of reference.

This book offers partial answers, not complete solutions. All content is drawn from public-domain sources; no classified material is included. What you conclude is your decision. While other patents and designs exist, they often mismatch observed UFO behavior. These systems described align with reported extraterrestrial craft operations.

If you are a hardened skeptic this is not the book for you. No matter how much convergence over time I show it will not be enough. There is no smoking gun here. If you are on the fence, keep reading.
